About Us

Expert Repairs

Our skilled students repair computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones with professional-grade tools and techniques.

How Pricing Works

Our labor is free but if there are any damaged parts, then the customer will have to order the new repair parts.

Example of parts and their prices:

Phone Screen($40 - $125), Battery($20 - $60), RAM($20 - $80), Laptop Screen($80 - $200)

Repair Time

Most repairs are completed within 2-7 school days. If there are any repair parts that the customer ordered then times may vary. We'll keep you updated on the process.

Ticketing & Workflow System

Each repair is logged into a ticketing system that tracks device details, reported issues, assigned technicians, repair status, and completion data. This ensures accountability, traceability, and organized repair within our workspace.

Where we're located

Our Classroom C220 can be located on the right side of the building right next to Joes Store

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services including computer repairs, laptop diagnostics, and smartphone fixes. We also provide data recovery and software installation services.
