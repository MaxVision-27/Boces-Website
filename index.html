About Us

Expert Repairs

Our skilled students repair computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones with professional-grade tools and techniques.

How Pricing Works

Our labor is free, but if any parts are damaged the customer will need to order the replacement parts.

Examples: Phone Screen ($40–$125), Battery ($20–$60), RAM ($20–$80), Laptop Screen ($80–$200)

Repair Time

Most repairs are completed within 2–7 school days. If parts need to be ordered, repair times may vary.

Ticketing & Workflow System

Each repair is logged into a ticketing system that tracks device details, reported issues, assigned technicians, repair status, and completion data.

Where We're Located

Our classroom (C220) is located on the right side of the building next to Joe's Store.

Our Services

We repair computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones, and also provide data recovery and software installation services.

AI Engineers

Artificial Intelligence is becoming a huge part of modern technology. Our team can help explain how AI works, how tools like ChatGPT generate responses, and how machine learning systems learn from data. Whether you're curious about AI or want to start learning about it, we're happy to help.

Warranty Education

We help customers understand how device warranties work and what they cover. Our team can explain when a repair may affect a warranty and help you determine the best repair option for your device.
